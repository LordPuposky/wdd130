White Water Rafting Company

Our Mission

At White Water Rafting Company, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on the water.

History

Founded in 2010 by a team of outdoor enthusiasts and whitewater experts, White Water Rafting Company began as a small family-run operation on the roaring rivers of Cundinamarca. What started with just two rafts and a passion for adventure has grown into one of South America’s most trusted rafting outfitters. Our journey has been fueled by an unwavering commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and delivering unforgettable experiences that push boundaries while respecting nature’s power.

Over a decade of excellence, we’ve pioneered safety protocols now adopted industry-wide, with our certified guides leading over 2,000 expeditions without a major incident. As proud members of the International Rafting Federation, we combine thrills with responsibility 5% of every trip funds local river conservation, and since 2018, we’ve operated 100% plastic-free. Our trips don’t just showcase nature’s beauty; they actively protect it for future adventurers.
